# Financial Intelligence Portfolio

## Overview

This portfolio demonstrates the development of enterprise-grade financial intelligence solutions designed to help SMEs transform operational and financial data into actionable business decisions.

Each project follows a complete analytics lifecycle—from data preparation through predictive modelling and executive reporting—to simulate real-world consulting engagements.

The portfolio focuses on building scalable financial intelligence systems that improve profitability, reduce operational risk, strengthen cash flow visibility, and support better strategic decision-making.

# Portfolio Projects

## Financial Health Scoring

Developed a proprietary Financial Health Score that combines multiple operational and financial indicators into a single business health metric.

The framework evaluates:

* Profitability
* Profit Margin
* Revenue Stability
* Payment Behaviour
* Customer Retention
* Churn Risk
* Client Tenure
* Customer Satisfaction
* Upselling Activity

The resulting score supports SME monitoring and provides an additional decision-support tool for alternative lenders and financial institutions.
